About Recommendations

The recommendation system uses a Random Forest algorithm to analyze student performance across different chapters and their dependencies.

Based on the analysis, students are categorized into one of these recommendation groups:

  • Special Class (>90): Student has exceptional understanding and may benefit from advanced material
  • Unnecessary (80-90): Student has good understanding and additional classes are not necessary
  • Required (70-80): Student would benefit from additional classes
  • Very Necessary (<70): Student requires additional classes to improve understanding

The system considers chapter dependencies to provide more accurate recommendations.
